Ascension

At Prestige level >20 and 40 you can ascend. Ascension reset Prestige to 1 but you will get 2*Ascension_level effect from prestige skill (stack multi x2 x4) and increase Automator Level by 30% (stack additive)

  • Ascension 1 Level Required: 20, Prestige effect: 100% (normal)
  • Ascension 2 Level Required: 40, Prestige effect: 200%
  • Ascension 3 Level Required: 80, Prestige effect: 400%
  • Ascension 4 Level Required: 160, Prestige effect: 600%
