Prestige

Prestige tab is unlocked by completely defeated the first enemy (all 100 cells). Prestige will give you Skill point based on max enemy level. To get more Skills you will need to defeat higher level enemy. By defeating enemy of level >=2 you will gain Inactive Dark Matter, prestige activate the Dark Matter. Dark Matter can be used to time warp; one unit of Dark Matter equals to one second.
Prestige will also unlock Automators.
