Laboratory

In the Laboratory you can research and improve new technologies. Research is generated by unused computing power. Researches in the "In Progress" column are researched, those in the "Backlog" not.

Starting research

What to focus when starting:

  • Better Researching: The first level can boost your research progress
  • Energy: You probably want more energy, so get some level of this
  • Corvette: This unlock a lot of stuff, but before this focus on Metal, Crystal and Energy production.
  • Scavenger: This is usefully if you play active and can take advantage of extra resource, you will need storage.
  • Frigate: Unlock a new ship and add 7 max naval capacity. Free naval capacity is good.
  • Warriors: When you need more naval capacity.
  • Telescope: After defeating the first enemy you will need this to find more powerfully enemies.
